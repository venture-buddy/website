Core Values

  • Mission Driven

    “Transform Tokyo to the next Silicon Valley” – yes, our mission has a big impact in society. Be the captain of the mission that nobody has taken on before. Evangelize and engage people to make things possible that we never imagined. Even though at hard times, let’s not forget the world we want to be in.

  • Be A Global Citizen

    We are challenging the long-term closemindedness of the Japanese ecosystem. That’s why we should be a role model by being a global citizen. Rather you speak English or not let’s think globally and actively welcoming diversity.

  • User First

    A customer centric service always comes from the customer’s challenges. Listen to your customer carefully, dig deep, implement, and get honest feedback. These are crucial to create a truly customer centric service.

  • Agile

    To achieve something big, we need to make the necessary mistakes quickly as possible until we get to the right spot. Agile organizations learns fast and ones who learn the fastest are the winners.

  • Thorough

    Sometimes a mistake may seem other people’s falt. But instead, ask yourself, “how could I acted to made things better?” A route of a problem can be found by thiking thoroughly and produce great outcomes in the future.

  • Trust, Honesty

    In a real sense, one founder is not enough to realize our vision. The Vision is universal enough for any member in the company would wanted from within. We shold all work because we want to work.

  • Curiosity

    We can only think from what we know. Learning helps to expand your frame of thought. Keep your personal growth going by learning new things and then bring back that to your team.

  • Think Big

    Don’t limit yourself in a small frame of thinking, great results won’t come with small thinking. Set bold policies and directions to achieve something big.
